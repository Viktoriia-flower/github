About_me:

Labrador Retriever, Labrador or just Lab for short… they’re the ultimate furry family member! It’s no surprise these pooches are one of the most popular breeds amongst American families - they’re bursting with bags of energy with an inspiring zest for life and enthusiasm for all things fun. Originally hunting dogs from Newfoundland, these outgoing pooches are avid swimmers with a passion for fetch and swimming. These cuties are at their best when surrounded by a loving pack who have the time for (almost endless) games of fetch, splash abouts in the river, and teaching new tricks. Labrador Retrievers really are the definition of an ‘all-rounder’ (unless you’re looking for a pooch you can fit under your arm, of course). Ideal for active families, they’re also successful working dogs. These goal-oriented cuties are popular in therapy settings and make for great service partners
